Quoizel Abner 5-Light Matte Black Chandelier

Elevate your space with the Quoizel Abner 5-light chandelier, a stunning fusion of traditional craftsmanship and modern industrial design. Its elegant, timeless matte black finish adds a sophisticated touch to any room, blending seamlessly with minimalistic, mid-century, and transitional decor styles.

Versatile Design for Any Room

The Abner chandelier is ideal for creating the perfect ambiance in dining rooms, living areas, bedrooms, bars, or entryways. Its sleek silhouette and modern appeal make it a versatile lighting solution that adapts to your interior needs with ease.

Durable and Reliable Construction

Engineered with high-quality steel, this chandelier is built to last, offering sturdiness and resilience for long-term use. Its adjustable height, up to 77 inches, allows for custom installation to fit your space perfectly, whether it's a sloped ceiling or a damp environment.

Featuring a dimmable function, the Abner chandelier allows you to control the lighting intensity, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere. It's compatible with candelabra base bulbs, making it easy to install and maintain while adding a touch of refined style to your home.

Elegant and Functional Lighting Solution

The Quoizel Abner 5-light chandelier brings a unique charm to your decor, combining modern industrial design with a contemporary twist. It's not only visually striking but also practical, offering flexibility and durability for both residential and commercial spaces.
