Elegant Flameless Candles with Glitter Finish

FURORA LIGHTING presents a pack of 3 compact glitter flameless candles, crafted to bring warmth and charm to your wedding or event decor. Each candle features a lifelike flickering effect that creates a cozy, inviting atmosphere without the risks associated with real flames. With a petite size of 2.4 inches wide and 1.6 inches high, they are easy to place on trays or displays, making them perfect for centerpieces or decorative accents.

These candles are designed with simplicity and safety in mind. They come with 3 pre-installed CR2032 batteries so you can enjoy their glowing features right out of the box. There's no timer, so you have complete manual control to turn them on and off as needed. The shimmering finish adds a touch of luxury, making them a standout addition to your event or home decor.

Whether you're planning a romantic dinner, a special celebration, or simply want to enhance the ambiance of your living space, these LED candles are a versatile and reliable choice. Battery-operated and flameless, they are safe for use around children, pets, and flammable materials, ensuring worry-free enjoyment for all occasions.
